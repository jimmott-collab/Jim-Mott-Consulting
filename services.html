For all the tools and speed available to us — things are getting more abstract and less clear. The more detached from the data we become, the harder it is to develop the gut feel that’s needed when there are challenging decisions to be made.

That gut feel comes from having made a connection to something authentic, unexpected and usefully new. Good research provides this by going beyond the expected patterns in people’s behaviour, finding the rough edges and giving them room to breathe.
